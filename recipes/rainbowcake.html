Rainbow Cake

Description

A delicious rainbow cake that can be used for birthdays, holidays, or any time!

Ingredients

For rainbow cake layers

  • Nonstick cooking spray
  • 2 (16.25 oz.) boxes white cake mix
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 6 large egs
  • Gel food coloring in red, yellow, green, and blue

For decorating

  • 2 containers (about 3 cups) white or vanilla frosting
  • About 2 cups rainbow sprinkles
