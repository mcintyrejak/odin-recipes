Ingredients

  • Sticky rice, either leftover or fresh
  • Shelled edamame
  • Sakura denbu
  • Umeboshi
  • Shishamo

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice using your preferred method and scoop into your chosen lunch box. The rice should almost completely fill the box
  2. Spoon cooked edamame into roughly 1/5 of the box, keeping it in a neat group.
  3. Using the shishamo, divide the lunch box in half.
  4. Sprinkle the sakura denbu on half the rice.
  5. Place the umeboshi in the center of the other half of the rice.
