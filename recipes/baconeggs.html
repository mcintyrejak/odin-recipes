Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Eggs

Instructions

  1. Heat a pan of your choosing over low to medium heat.
  2. Add the bacon, making sure not to crowd the pan with too many strips at a time.
  3. Cook the bacon to your liking. The slower the better.
  4. When the bacon is done, remove it from the pan to drain on a paper towel. Repeat with the rest of the strips of bacon.
  5. Add the eggs, being careful not to break the yolks.
  6. When the eggs are sufficiently fried, add them to a plate with a few strips of bacon.
